Our Mission

What do we do?
We connect many eager students ready to work on medical devices or their own projects and we are prepared to facilitate their journey to do so.


We believe in growth by experience.
A student-organized incubator for creative thinkers and an innovative inventors. We give students real-world experience in engineering, design, market realization, project management, and teamwork.
